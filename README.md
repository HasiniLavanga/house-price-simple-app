## 📊 Business Insights

- Data-driven pricing improves business decisions
---

## 📈 Hypothesis Testing

### Business Hypothesis

H0: House area does not significantly affect house price.

H1: House area significantly affects house price.

### Statistical Test Used

Pearson Correlation Test

### Conclusion

Since p-value < 0.05, the null hypothesis is rejected.

House area significantly impacts house price.
